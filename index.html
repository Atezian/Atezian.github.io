About me

With over 10 years of experience in composing and 4 years in sound design, I have cultivated a well of knowledge that allows me to work efficiently and effectively.

An important concept I've come to understand is that sound design should accentuate what is happening visually. If the audio stands out too much on its then it will bring the listener out of their suspension of disbelief. You know audio is working well when it isn't questioned or noticed separately from the screenplay.

My passion is composing vibrant and unique pieces that evoke an emotional response in the listener. In my experience as a listener, it is a special moment when you hear a moving piece of music for the first time. Due to drawing influence from a wide array of music, I understand a variety of genres and timbres, and I strive to reflected this in my work.
